Christopher Jones

Technologist

Scouter & Foodie

The master of

the art of living

makes little

distinction

between his

work & play,

labor & leisure,

mind & body,

education & recreation,

love & religion.

He simply pursues his vision of

excellence

in whatever he does,

leaving others

to decide whether he is working or playing.

To him, he is

always doing both.
